Allergens

In case of any kind of food allergy or hypersensitivity to any ingredient please advise our colleagues before ordering. We will do all we can to accommodate these requirements, though please note that certain alterations may not be possible. Our foods may contain traces of additional allergens in addition to allergens labeled as ingredients.
